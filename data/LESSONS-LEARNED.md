## Content & Format Insights

### What works
1. **"Stop [X wrong]" imperative hooks are #1 on @yournotetaker.** Confirmed again with fresh data — contrast 6-slide posts avg 184.5 views (224, 216, 171, 127). This is 2x every other hook.
2. **6 slides > 8-9 slides for VIEWS.** 6-slide posts avg 184 views vs 8-9 slide avg ~100 on @yournotetaker. Shorter = better completion rate = algorithm boost.
3. **8 slides best for SAVES.** 1.04-1.08% save rate on 8-slide bold_claim — saves signal value.
4. **Stat-lead hooks with numbers work as mid-tier.** ~109 views avg. Reliable but not top-tier.
5. **Bold_claim hooks = best save rate.** 0.61% avg save rate across 6 posts. Reliable for saves, not views.
6. **Animated flow is the only flow that works.** Zero photorealistic posts broke 80 views.
7. **Question hooks show promise.** 127 views on single data point. Needs validation — could be #2 best hook.

### What doesn't work
8. **story_opener hooks are DEAD.** Max 18 views across all tests. Do not use on any account.
9. **1-4 slide posts are dead.** No post with fewer than 6 slides ever broke 80 views.
10. **Photorealistic flow fails completely.** Blends into feed, looks like stock.
11. **Duplicate captions tank both posts.** TikTok suppresses duplicate content.

### @miniutewise_thomas specific
12. **Thomas has 350 views baseline.** His audience is much larger (1737 followers vs 188). Every post gets 300-400 views.
13. **Our contrast 6-slide posts perform at Thomas's baseline** — 349 avg views vs 362 for his existing posts. Not outperforming yet.
14. **Thomas's audience saves more.** Top save rate 1.83% (7 saves on 383 views). Higher than any @yournotetaker post.
15. **Need Thomas-specific experiments.** What beats his 350 baseline? Try bold_claim or question hooks.

### View tiers (@yournotetaker)
16. **200+ views = contrast hook + 6 slides + emotional CTA.** Formula: "Stop [bad behavior]" + tips + "Save for exam week."
17. **100-150 views = stat/number hook + 8-9 slides.** Educational curiosity drives consistent mid-tier.
18. **<20 views = story_opener or 1-4 slides.** Dead zone. Don't bother.
